All About Gundam Model kits!!!

Gundam kits are plastic kits you put together to make a figure that you can articulate into different poses. And the most notable gundam kits are Gunpla which are made in Japan.

What is a gundam?

A gundam is a fictional robot suit that people can control. it is from an anime series called Mobile Gundam Suit.
